Get Involved

Wear Your Kind is built on community. Whether you have time, skills, or resources to share, there are real ways to make an impact.

You don’t need experience. You don’t need a title. You just need to show up with intention.

Community Outreach

Help us build connections across Chicago and strengthen our network of support.

  • Contact churches, shelters, and community centers
  • Reach out to local media and share our story
  • Help build relationships with partners and supporters

Events & Fundraising

Support the planning and execution of events that move the mission forward.

  • Help organize and set up fundraising events
  • Search for venues and coordinate logistics
  • Brainstorm new fundraising ideas
  • Assist in securing support from businesses and donors

Creative & Media

Use your creativity to help us grow, communicate, and reach more people.

  • Create short marketing videos and digital content
  • Help manage social media and outreach campaigns
  • Write content, emails, and updates
  • Support storytelling and communications

Operations & Support

Help keep the work moving behind the scenes and on the ground.

  • Accept and organize donations
  • Assist with budgeting or accounting
  • Purchase and manage clothing inventory
  • Support website updates and maintenance
  • Write thank-you notes to contributors

Ready to Help?

Whether you have time, skills, or resources to offer, there’s a place for you here. Every contribution helps move this mission forward.
