Current Events

May 2nd, 2026

Annual Englewood Unity Day with Imagine Englewood If



Wear Your Kind is proud to partner with Imagine Englewood If as a sponsor for this year’s Englewood Unity Day. On May 2, our team will trade in business attire for casual clothes, roll up our sleeves, and join the effort to help clean and revitalize blocks in Englewood.



We’re excited to support this community-driven event, build connections, and contribute to a positive impact. We hope this will become an annual tradition.

May 2026

WYK Clothing Giveaway



We’re also pleased to announce one of our first partnerships with Olive Branch Mission. Together, we will provide 50 individual clothing kits with brand-new essentials to support Olive Branch clients as they head into spring.



This is just the beginning of what we hope will be an ongoing effort to serve the community. Stay tuned for more details.

$10,000 = 50 people clothed.
