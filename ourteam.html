About Us

“Life is not about finding yourself, it’s about creating yourself.”

Sabrina Murmigotti

President

Born in Milan, Italy, she moved to the United States at age 20 and spent 13 years in Chicago. That experience profoundly shaped her, helping her adapt to new settings, embrace different perspectives, and build meaningful cross-cultural relationships. It was also in Chicago that she raised her daughter, now a brilliant woman and one of her greatest sources of joy.

Professionally, she built a strong career in sales, successfully managing and growing businesses for leading tile companies. Working in dynamic and competitive environments strengthened her ability to build trust, understand people, and deliver consistent results.

She is someone who feels deeply and finds genuine joy in connection. She values meaningful conversations, intentional listening, and authentic, natural relationships. She believes people enter our lives for a reason and is often struck by how they contribute to her growth.

Her curiosity and love for life drive her to explore—through travel, nature, and new experiences. At the same time, she values stillness and the moments that allow her to remain present and grounded.

She lives with intention, caring for herself through healthy nutrition, connection with nature, and continuous learning. These are not just habits, but commitments that keep her balanced, energized, and aligned.

She believes in contributing to a better world, knowing that even the smallest actions can have a meaningful impact, and in giving back to society in ways that matter.

At her core, she strives to live with presence and purpose—embracing life fully, building meaningful connections, and moving forward with curiosity and openness toward what’s next.

“Connection is every thing and how we show up for one another truly matters.”

Destiny Fox

Vice President of Marketing & Fundraising

Born in Northern Italy in 1989, Destiny Nicole Fox was raised between cultures, shaping a life rooted in connection, perspective, and adaptability. After moving to the United States as an infant, she grew up in Chicago, surrounded by a vibrant and diverse community that helped form her open mindset and strong sense of identity.

At 14, Destiny returned to Italy, where she reconnected deeply with her roots and embraced the language and culture with ease. This bicultural experience became one of her greatest strengths, allowing her to move seamlessly between worlds and build meaningful, authentic connections with people from all walks of life.

At her core, Destiny is both creative and intuitive. She has a deeply artistic soul, expressed through painting, music, dance, poetry, and songwriting, each outlet reflecting emotional depth, curiosity, and a thoughtful perspective on the world around her.

Alongside her creative spirit is a strong entrepreneurial drive. Destiny is motivated by more than personal success; she is guided by a genuine desire to uplift others and create impact through her work. Known for her warmth and authenticity, she has a natural ability to make people feel seen, heard, and understood.

“Endeavor to do your best.”

Grant Gallinger

Vice President of Finance

Grant is a dedicated family man, business leader, and passionate motorsports enthusiast who brings energy, creativity, and a strong sense of service to everything he does. Married and the proud father of two, Grant currently resides in the northwest suburbs of Chicago.

As Director of Sales – Midwest for MCE Automation in the industrial process flow control industry, Grant has spent over two decades leading sales teams. He is recognized for his strategic vision, his ability to incubate profitable business models, and his talent for delivering innovative technical solutions and actionable results.

Outside of his professional life, he is an active member of his church and regularly participates in mission trips. He is an Illinois Freemason and immensely proud of his family history. Grant is a United Empire Loyalist, and his great-grandfather even won the world’s first documented automobile race in 1878.

A lifelong dog lover, Grant is also a connoisseur of fine bourbons, collects and shaves with antique straight razors, and restores historic British cars/motorcycles. His greatest passion is motorcycle racing, where he competes in superbike events with ASRA and serves as a control rider for MotoVid in the Midwest.

He also enjoys studying military and world history, taking extended road trips, and has driven the entire Route 66.

Grant’s diverse interests and commitment to family, faith, and community reflect his belief in living life fully while giving back.

“Nothing is impossible. Look at the word; it says ‘I’m possible.’”

Jessica Holmes

Director of Charitable Associations

Jessica has over 25 years of hospitality experience. She hails from the south side of Chicago and is a proud product of CPS schools. She received her culinary degree from Le Cordon Bleu in Chicago and obtained her bachelor’s degree from Robert Morris University.

She has held management positions with Bravo Restaurants, Levy Restaurants, and Compass Group. She has also held culinary roles at the Chicago Firehouse, Horseshoe Casino, and Fox & Obel.

After receiving her MBA from Saint Xavier University in 2014, she began teaching there before transitioning to Kennedy King College’s Washburne Culinary School in 2015. She is also the co-founder of SoulBoricua LLC, a catering and event provider as well as a food service product distribution company focused on employing and training individuals in underserved communities.

Through this work, she developed Holmes Hospitality, a nonprofit consulting brand that partners with organizations to support community development by equipping individuals with practical skills and training. Her collaborations include the Greater Chicago Food Depository, Chicago Botanic Garden, Teamwork Englewood, and Growing Home.

“Make yourself useful.”

Zachary Romero

Social Media Platforms Director

Zachary was born and raised in the Chicagoland area and has spent time both in the city and downstate Illinois, gaining experience with a wide range of people and different social environments. He studied Arabic in college and has maintained a lasting interest in language, culture, and the ways many people make sense of the world around them.

Much of his professional background has been in pawn shops, where he developed a strong ability for reading people, assessing value, and thinking practically in fast-moving situations. He has also worked in property management, which added a more structured and operational dimension to his experience, while reinforcing the importance of consistency, accountability, and follow-through.

At his core, Zachary is drawn to what is honest, functional, and worth understanding on a deeper level. He values curiosity, authenticity, and a grounded perspective on how things work, and he enjoys meaningful conversations, learning from others, and finding substance beneath the surface. That same thoughtful nature extends into his personal life, including a genuine love for cats. Their intelligence, independence, and quiet companionship resonate with him, reflecting a softer side and a deep appreciation for connection, character, and introspection.

“Walking by faith, winning by design.”

Rochelle Cowan

Media Production & PR Coverage Director

Rochelle Cowan is a multifaceted creative professional and administrative leader with a strong foundation in both organizational operations and media production.

She currently serves as an Administrative Assistant within a church environment, where she brings strategic coordination, operational excellence, and a people-centered approach to everything she does.

In addition to her administrative work, Rochelle is a dedicated film producer and writer with a passion for storytelling that reflects real-life experiences, culture, and community. Her creative work focuses on developing compelling visual content and narratives that resonate with diverse audiences.

Rochelle is an active contributor to Chicago 365 / Urban Access TV, a Chicago-based media platform committed to showcasing the heartbeat of the city. Through this platform, she helps highlight Chicago’s entertainment, nightlife, food, fashion, business, sports, film, and community events.

The organization specializes in event coverage, social media promotion, post-event highlights, and creating brand visibility for partners and sponsors. She is also a member of Crew Call Collective, a collaborative network that connects creatives across the film and entertainment industry—including directors, producers, actors, writers, makeup artists, and behind-the-scenes professionals. Through this collective, Rochelle continues to build meaningful relationships and contribute to the growth of Chicago’s creative community.

With a unique blend of administrative expertise and creative vision, Rochelle continues to build platforms, produce meaningful content, and drive initiatives that connect communities and elevate creative voices.
