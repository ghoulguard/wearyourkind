About Us

Wear Your Kind is a Chicago-based initiative focused on delivering essential, high-quality clothing to individuals experiencing homelessness. What started as a simple idea—helping people meet a basic need—has grown into a hands-on, community-driven effort to restore dignity, warmth, and a sense of belonging.

We organize community-driven efforts to deliver weather-appropriate clothing directly to those who need it most. Through local partnerships, volunteers, and targeted initiatives, we turn small acts of kindness into tangible support for our community.

We’re starting in Chicago, with the goal of building something that can grow, connect communities, and expand its reach over time.
